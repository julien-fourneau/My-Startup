Our mission

Trivial Charter is a start-up that is focused on creating engaging and entertaining trivia experiences. Whether you're a fan of trivia nights, board games, or digital quizzes, Trivial Charter has something for you. Our mission is to make trivia more accessible and enjoyable for everyone, whether you're a trivia novice or a seasoned pro. Our team is dedicated to developing innovative and exciting trivia content, as well as providing top-notch event planning and educational resources. With Trivial Charter, you can expect to have fun, learn something new, and connect with others who share your love of trivia.
